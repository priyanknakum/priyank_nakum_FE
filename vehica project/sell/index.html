Documents required to sell used cars online?

  • Registration Certificate (RC):
    • The RC is the most important document required to sell a used car. It is a legal document that proves ownership of the car.

  • Insurance Policy:
    • The insurance policy is another important document required to sell a used car. It proves that the car is insured and that the buyer will be covered in case of an accident.

  • Any other relevant documents:
    • Depending on the state in which you are selling the car, you may need to provide other documents, such as a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the financier or a letter from the previous owner.
